View on the Job

Philips

* Philips always looks to understand the customer needs and prioritize active engagement and innovative solutions to give the customers the best possible experience and the highest levels of satisfaction.
* Five job opportunities at that organization are: Head of Data Transformation, Senior Director Data Transformation, Director in Data and Innovation Programs, Head of Data Transformation, IT Market Lead Israel
* The technologies this organization uses are: Rapid equipment deployment kit to COVID-19, major extensions to its industry-leading Azurion image-guided therapy platform.

Dell

* Dell on the other hand prioritizes his workers by giving them flexibility and a balanced work-life experience.
* Five job opportunities at that organization are: Product designer, Implemantation consultant, Architect – Java, Desktop support analyst, cloud architect.
* Dell's products include personal computers, servers, smartphones, televisions, computer software, computer security and network security, as well as information security services.

Microsoft

* Microsoft has a similar approach to Dell however they concentrate on having diversity in their working environment as that guarantees all kinds off different ideas from all kinds of backgrounds.
* Five job opportunities at that organization are: Data Platform Engineer, IT Solution Manager, Manager IT Solution Management, IT Service Operations Microsoft Edge web platform Microsoft Azure
* Microsoft’s technologies include personal computers, servers, smartphones, televisions, and computer software.

Huawei

Your Surprice

This company is on top of my list and here is why:
The work atmosphere there looks very pleasant. I found the company to be with young
and open culture that encourages everyone to contribute to the overall success with their
own ideas. The flat hierarchy, I believe (As I have noticed similar situations in the university),
makes it easy to talk to CEOs about their own development or general work. Interns are paid
according to general rate, which is generous as that is volunteering. All of these qualities
make it the perfect company for a young motivated and creative person like me.

Macaw

This company is my second choice on this list and here is why:
From what I have gathered Macaw works mostly with the latest technology.
Has good collaboration with colleagues and If you need help, everyone would
be there for you. Also, they help their customers with a high-quality solution,
thus building a long-term relationship with the customer. I also appreciated the
diverse culture they have in that company as I said already, I find it very important
to be diverse in a company in order to have many different ideas. These qualities
give the employee the opportunity to build strong connections in the working environment
however I believe it is more specialized in people who already have experience.

Vera code

This company is my third choice on this list and here is why:
I found Veracode to be a good place to work with good environment for learning new technologies,
mentors who are always there to help which I find very important to have in a company, and also
they have flexible work hours. However I did not find them particularly diverse or open-minded
which in my believe is not the best work culture.
